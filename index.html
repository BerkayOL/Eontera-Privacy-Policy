Eontera Gizlilik Politikasi

Bu metin, Eontera uygulamasinin bilgileri nasil isledigini aciklar. Eontera, dogal olaylari ve yorge baglamini kamuya acik kaynaklardan derleyen bagimsiz ve bilgi amacli bir Android uygulamasidir.

1. Eontera ne yapar

Eontera; deprem, orman yangini, firtina, volkan, sel ve benzeri dogal olaylara iliskin kamuya acik verileri etkilesimli bir harita uzerinde gosterir. Uygulama ayrica istege bagli uydu takibi ozellikleri ile gorunur veri baglamina dayali ozetler sunabilir.

Onemli: Eontera resmi bir afet uyarisi, acil mudahale, kriz koordinasyon veya tehlike tahmin hizmeti degildir. Guvenlik ve acil durum kararlarinda her zaman resmi yerel makamlarin ve yetkili kurumlarin aciklamalari esas alinmalidir.

2. Isledigimiz bilgiler

2.1 Istege bagli cihaz konumu

Kullanici approximate location izni verirse, Eontera bu bilgiyi yalnizca ISS veya benzeri gokyuzu baglami sunan ozelliklerde gozlemci baglamini hesaplamak icin kullanir.

  • Yalnizca approximate (coarse) konum kullanilir.
  • Kullanim foreground ile sinirlidir; background location kullanilmaz.
  • Konum izni zorunlu degildir; ana harita deneyimi izinsiz de calisabilir.
  • Kamera kullanilmaz; cihaz yonelim sensorleri ve approximate konum yeterlidir.

2.2 Watch-zone verileri

Kullanici bir watch zone olusturdugunda, secilen bolge adi, koordinatlar ve yaricap bilgisi cihazin uygulamaya ozel depolama alaninda yerel olarak tutulur.

  • Depolama alani Android uygulama sandbox'i icindeki yerel veritabani yapisidir.
  • Bu veriler uygulama duzeyinde encrypted degildir.
  • Watch-zone verileri herhangi bir uzak hesaba veya backend servisine yuklenmez.
  • Veriler siz silene, uygulama verisini temizleyene veya uygulamayi kaldirana kadar kalabilir.

2.3 Yerel onbellek ve tercihler

Eontera performans, gecici goruntuleme ve tercih yonetimi icin belirli verileri cihaz uzerinde yerel olarak saklayabilir.

  • Kamuya acik kaynaklardan cekilen son olay verileri yerel onbellekte tutulabilir.
  • Bildirim tercihleri, karsilama durumu ve arayuz ayarlari cihazda saklanabilir.
  • Harita kutuphaneleri performans amacli gecici tile onbelleklemesi yapabilir.

2.4 Ag meta verileri

Eontera kamuya acik veri kaynaklarina HTTPS istekleri gonderirken, saglayicilar normal ag isletimi kapsaminda belirli meta verileri gorebilir.

  • IP adresi
  • Istek zamani
  • HTTP user-agent basligi
  • Talep edilen URL

Bu meta veriler ilgili ucuncu taraf saglayicilar tarafindan kendi gizlilik kosullari kapsaminda islenebilir. Eontera bu isleme surecini kontrol etmez.

3. Toplamadigimiz bilgiler

Eontera asagidaki veri turlerini toplamaz, hesap sistemi icinde islemez veya reklam amacli kullanmaz:

  • Ad, e-posta adresi, telefon numarasi veya hesap bilgileri
  • Precise location veya background location
  • Kamera, mikrofon, fotograf veya video verileri
  • Kisiler, takvim veya arama verileri
  • Finansal bilgi, odeme bilgisi veya IAP verisi
  • Reklam tanimlayicilari veya analytics SDK verileri
  • Kullanici tarafindan olusturulan sosyal icerik
  • Uzak hesap kimlikleri veya hesabin silinmesini gerektiren profil verileri

English text

Eontera Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Eontera handles information. Eontera is an independent informational Android app that presents public Earth-event and orbital context.

1. What Eontera does

Eontera displays publicly available data about earthquakes, wildfires, storms, volcanoes, floods, and related natural events on an interactive map. It may also provide optional satellite tracking features and summaries derived from visible public data.

Important: Eontera is not an official disaster warning service, emergency response service, crisis coordination service, or guaranteed hazard-prediction product. For safety decisions, users should always rely on official local authorities and emergency institutions.

2. Information we handle

2.1 Optional device location

If the user grants approximate location permission, Eontera may use that information only to calculate observer context for ISS or similar sky-related experiences.

  • Approximate coarse location only.
  • Foreground use only; no background location is requested or used.
  • Location permission is optional and not required for the main map experience.
  • The camera is not used; orientation sensors and approximate location are sufficient.

2.2 Watch-zone data

When a user creates a watch zone, the selected location name, coordinates, and radius are stored locally in the app's private device storage area.

  • Stored inside the Android app sandbox through local database structures.
  • This data is not app-level encrypted.
  • Watch-zone data is not uploaded to a remote account or backend service.
  • It may remain until the user deletes it, clears app data, or uninstalls the app.

2.3 Local cache and preferences

Eontera may keep certain data locally on the device for performance, temporary viewing, and preference management.

  • Recently fetched public event data may be kept in local cache.
  • Notification choices, onboarding state, and interface settings may be stored on-device.
  • Map libraries may maintain temporary tile caching for performance.

2.4 Network metadata

When Eontera sends HTTPS requests to public data sources, those providers may observe standard network metadata as part of normal network operations.

  • IP address
  • Request timestamp
  • HTTP user-agent header
  • Requested URL

Those providers may process that metadata under their own privacy terms. Eontera does not control that separate processing.

3. What we do not collect

Eontera does not collect or use the following data categories for account, advertising, or profiling purposes:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or account credentials
  • Precise location or background location
  • Camera, microphone, photo, or video data
  • Contacts, calendar data, or call records
  • Financial information, payment information, or IAP data
  • Advertising identifiers or analytics SDK telemetry
  • User-generated social content
  • Remote profile data that would require account deletion workflows

4. Third-party services

Eontera may connect to publicly available HTTPS data providers. These references identify data sources only and do not imply any official relationship or endorsement.

5. Data sharing

Eontera does not sell user data. It also does not transfer user data to third parties for advertising, behavioral targeting, or analytics profiling.

However, standard network metadata may still be visible to the public data providers as part of ordinary HTTPS communication.

6. Data security

  • HTTPS is used for supported network communication.
  • Local data is protected by the Android application sandbox.
  • Watch-zone and similar local data is not app-level encrypted.
  • No secret keys or tokens are intentionally stored in user-accessible locations.

8. Children's privacy

Eontera is not designed for children under 13. It is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Notifications

If the user opts in to the relevant feature, Eontera may display local informational notifications. These notifications are not official emergency alerts and do not create a guarantee.
